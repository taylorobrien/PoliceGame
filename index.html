Police Escape

  • High Concept: You are trapped with no gun. Find the key, the gun, and get out.
  • Player Experience Goals:The player should feel the game simple, but challenging due to the amount of the zombies coming after them, especially with no gun.
  • Genre: Escape Game
  • Game play:In this prototype of Police Escape, the player uses the arrows to move around and click to shoot. In order to shoot, the character must first find the gun, located on the map. After finding the gun, the player can now eliminate the zombies. There is a key on the map that must be collected in order to exit the level, which is done by the player entering the door.
  • Prototype goal:This prototype is evaluating object collision, using groups that involve spritesheets, larger maps, physics, and shooting.
  • Features: The Music, and the shooting effects
  • Setting/Premise: The player is trapped inside a room.
  • Story: The game is based off of the Walking Dead.
  • Target Audience: This game is intended for new-gamers, or gamers that enjoy simple game.
  • Play time: 1-3 minutes
  • Strategies: Avoid the zombies, and retrieve gun as fast as possible.
